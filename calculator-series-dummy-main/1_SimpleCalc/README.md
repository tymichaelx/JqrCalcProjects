Simple Calculator in C
---
## Heading 2
How to Use:
./simplecalc (operand1) (operator) (operand2)

Supported Operators:
*  (+) addition
*  (-) subtraction
*  (*) multiplication
*  (/) divide
*  (%) modulo
*  (<<) left shift
*  (>>) right shift
*  (&) and
*  (|) or
*  (^) xor 
*  (<<<) rotate left
*  (>>>) rotate right

Operands:
Negative numbers not accepted for bit related equations (<<, >>, &, |, ^, <<<, >>>)
